Book a private group tour

Use this form to book a private group tour. These tours have a minimum cost, equivalent to the cost for seven adults.

If you are an individual or smaller group, we suggest taking a daily tour (no booking required).

Tour costs are in New Zealand dollars and include all taxes and fees – there are no additional charges. All tours depend on the availability of suitable staff.
