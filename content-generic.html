Guided tours 

Te Papa offers daily tours for individuals and small groups, plus private tours for larger groups. We also provide a visitor booklet and an audio guide.

Individuals and small groups – daily tours
Groups of seven or more – private group tours
Visitor booklet and audio guide

Individuals and small groups – daily tours

We offer a daily 60-minute tour – Introducing Te Papa.

Heading 3

This tour is ideal if you have limited time or want an overview before a longer visit. It:

Heading 4

  • is for individuals and small groups
  • is given in English
  • requires no booking – simply buy your ticket on the day at the Information Desk, Level 1.

If you would like a tour in a language other than English, please book a private group tour.

Groups of seven or more – private group tours

Book a private group tour tailored to your interests or special needs.

Private group tours have a minimum cost, equivalent to the cost for seven adults. If you are an individual or smaller group, we suggest taking a daily tour (no booking required).

We can deliver private group tours in English, Māori, French, German, Spanish, and Mandarin.

Visitor books and audio guide

Te Papa Explorer booklet

Use our visitor booklet, Te Papa Explorer, if you want to explore the Museum in your own time. This booklet includes maps showing where to find exhibitions. Buy it for $3 from the Information Desk, Level 2.

Te Papa: Your Essential Guide

Also available is the new publication Te Papa: Your Essential Guide. This compact book full of beautiful images is the complete guide to, and memento of, your visit to the Museum. It's available from the Information Desk, Level 2, and Te Papa Store, $14.99.
Buy Te Papa: Your Essential Guide online today

Audio guide

Our audio guide provides information about selected exhibitions and objects. It is available in English, Māori, and German and costs $5.
